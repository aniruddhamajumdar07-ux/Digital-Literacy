Social Media Do's and Don'ts

Do's:

* Think before you post. Check what you are saying to make sure it is correct and will not hurt anyone.
* Respect peoples privacy. Never share pictures or personal details of your friends without asking them
* Keep your media profiles clean. Make sure what you post will not stop you from getting a job or getting into a school.
* Be kind to others. Help your classmates by sharing things you think they will find useful. Join in on conversations.
* Make sure what you share is true. Check your facts before you post. This way you do not spread information.
* Take care of your health. Don't spend much time on social media. Take breaks when you need to.

Don'ts:

* Don't post when you are upset. It's not an idea to share angry messages or personal things when you are feeling emotional.
* Don't be mean to people online. Never try to hurt or embarrass someone on the internet.
* Don't share much about yourself. Be careful what you post. Don't tell people where you are all the time.
* Don't argue with people in public. Having fights with people on media can hurt your relationships and reputation.
* Don't use media in class or during a test. It can distract you. It can also make your teachers think you are not paying attention.
* Don't accept friend requests from people you don't know. Make sure you know who someone is before you accept their friend request,
